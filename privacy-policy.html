Privacy Policy for Won Of One

Last updated: 11 December 2025

Introduction

Won Of One ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App"). By using the App, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

1. Account Information

  • Email address
  • Name (first and last)
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Profile image (optional)
  • Account creation timestamp

2. Profile Information

  • Preferred weapon type
  • Handedness
  • Profile bio (optional)

3. Activity and Performance Data

  • Match data: scores, opponents, locations, notes, match events
  • Training logs: sessions, categories, targets, completion history
  • Goals and progress tracking
  • Statistics: streaks, win rates, performance summaries

4. Device and Technical Information

  • Device type, OS version, app version
  • App interactions and screen navigation
  • Error logs and crash diagnostics
  • Network connection status (online/offline)

5. User-Generated Content

  • Bug reports
  • Match notes
  • Goal descriptions

6. Subscription Information

  • Subscription status (active, expired, cancelled)
  • Subscription type (monthly or yearly)
  • Purchase records processed by Apple, Google, or RevenueCat

How We Use Your Information

1. Provide Core Services

  • Account management
  • Store, display, and sync training and match data
  • Generate progress analytics and performance summaries

2. Improve the App

  • Analyse feature usage
  • Resolve bugs and stability issues
  • Enhance overall performance

3. Communication

  • Password resets and account updates
  • Support communication
  • Important service announcements

4. Legal and Security

  • Fraud and abuse prevention
  • Compliance with legal obligations

Third-Party Services

Supabase

Database, authentication, and storage provider. GDPR compliant.

PostHog

Analytics and crash reporting (EU servers). Users may opt-out of analytics.

RevenueCat

Subscription and purchase management. GDPR compliant.

App Store / Google Play

Handles subscription payments and transactions.

Data Storage and Security

Storage Locations

  • Supabase Cloud (primary data storage)
  • PostHog (analytics)
  • Local device caching for offline functionality

Security Measures

  • Encrypted transmission (HTTPS)
  • Encrypted storage
  • Row-Level Security ensuring isolated user data
  • Routine security updates

Data Retention

  • Retained while the account is active
  • Deleted within 30 days after account deletion
  • Anonymised analytics may be stored longer

Your Rights and Choices

You may request a copy of your data at any time.

Correction

You may update your information directly in the App.

Deletion

You may delete your account in the App at any time.

Analytics Opt-Out

You may disable analytics in the settings page.
