--- layout: default title: Privacy Policy – Won Of One --- # Privacy Policy for Won Of One _Last updated: 11 December 2025_ ## Introduction Won Of One ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App"). Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using our App, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. ## Information We Collect ### 1. Account Information When you create an account, we collect: - **Email address** (required for account creation and authentication) - **Name** (first and last name, required for account creation) - **Password** (encrypted and securely stored by our authentication provider) - **Profile image/avatar** (optional, if you choose to upload one) - **Account creation date and time** ### 2. Profile Information You may optionally provide: - **Preferred weapon type** (epee, foil, sabre) - **Handedness** (left or right) - **Profile bio** (if you choose to add one) ### 3. Activity and Performance Data To provide our core services, we collect and store: - **Match data**: Scores, opponents, dates, locations, match types, duration, notes, and match events - **Training data**: Weekly training targets, session logs, activity types, and completion history - **Goals**: Goal types, target values, progress tracking, and completion status - **Match events**: Detailed timeline of match events including scores, cards, timer events, and period transitions - **Performance statistics**: Win rates, streaks, and other calculated metrics ### 4. Device and Technical Information We automatically collect certain technical information to improve the App: - **Device information**: Operating system, device model, app version, build number - **Usage data**: App interactions, feature usage, screen views, and navigation patterns - **Error and crash data**: Error logs, crash reports, and diagnostic information to help us fix bugs - **Network information**: Connection status (online/offline) for sync functionality ### 5. User-Generated Content - **Bug reports**: Descriptions, categories, steps to reproduce, and optional screenshots - **Match notes**: Notes you add to matches - **Goal descriptions**: Custom goal descriptions you create ### 6. Subscription Information If you subscribe to premium features: - **Subscription status**: Active, trial, expired, or cancelled - **Subscription type**: Monthly or yearly subscription - **Purchase information**: Processed through third-party payment providers (App Store, Google Play, RevenueCat) ## How We Use Your Information We use the information we collect to: ### 1. Provide Core Services - Create and manage your account - Store and display your match history, goals, and training data - Calculate and display statistics and progress - Enable offline functionality with data synchronization ### 2. Improve the App - Analyze usage patterns to improve features and user experience - Identify and fix bugs and technical issues - Monitor app performance and stability - Develop new features based on user needs ### 3. Communicate with You - Send account-related notifications (password resets, account updates) - Respond to bug reports and support requests - Send important updates about the App (if you opt in) ### 4. Legal and Security - Comply with legal obligations - Protect against fraud, abuse, and security threats - Enforce our Terms of Service ## Third-Party Services We use the following third-party services that may collect or process your data: ### 1. Supabase - **Purpose**: Database, authentication, and cloud storage - **Data**: All account information, match data, goals, and training data - **Location**: Data stored in secure cloud infrastructure - **Privacy**: Supabase is GDPR compliant. Learn more: ### 2. RevenueCat - **Purpose**: Subscription management and payment processing - **Data**: Subscription status, purchase information - **Privacy**: RevenueCat is GDPR compliant. Learn more: ### 4. App Store / Google Play - **Purpose**: App distribution and in-app purchase processing - **Data**: Purchase transactions, subscription management - **Privacy**: Governed by Apple and Google’s privacy policies ## Data Storage and Security ### Where Your Data is Stored - **Primary storage**: Supabase cloud database (PostgreSQL) - **Analytics data**: PostHog servers (EU region) - **Local storage**: Some data is cached locally on your device for offline functionality ### Security Measures - **Encryption**: Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest - **Authentication**: Secure password hashing and authentication via Supabase Auth - **Access controls**: Row-Level Security (RLS) ensures users can only access their own data - **Regular security updates**: We regularly update our dependencies and security practices ### Data Retention - **Active accounts**: We retain your data as long as your account is active - **Deleted accounts**: When you delete your account, we delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal purposes - **Analytics data**: Aggregated, anonymized analytics data may be retained longer for statistical purposes ## Your Rights and Choices ### Access and Export You have the right to: - Access your personal data - Export your data in a portable format - Request a copy of your data by contacting us ### Correction You can update your account information at any time through the App’s profile settings. ### Deletion You can delete your account and all associated data: - Through the App: **Settings → Delete Account** - By contacting us directly ### Analytics Opt-Out You can opt out of analytics tracking: - Through the App: **Settings → Analytics** (if available) - Note: Some basic error tracking may continue to function for app stability ### Data Portability You can request an export of your data in a machine-readable format by contacting us. ## Children’s Privacy Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13.
